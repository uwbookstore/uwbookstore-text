How to Enable Pop-ups

Enabling Pop-up Windows in Internet Explorer

Pop-ups must be allowed in your web browser and other installed pop-up blocking programs including third-party toolbars (e.g., Google, Yahoo!, AOL) for the University Book Store to display course content, important messages, and Help.

To allow pop-ups in IE for text.uwbookstore.com

  1. Go to Tools(if you do not see Tools, press the Alt key to show the menu bar)> Popup Blocker and Select Pop-up Blocker Settings.
  2. In the Address of web site field, type https://text.uwbookstore.com/

    Then click Add.

    The URL will appear in the list of allowed sites.
  3. Click Close.

Enabling Pop-up Windows in Firefox (Mac/Windows)

Pop-ups must be allowed in your web browser and other installed pop-up blocking programs including third-party toolbars (e.g., Google, Yahoo!, AOL) for the University Book Store to display course content, important messages, and Help.

To allow pop-ups in Firefox for text.uwbookstore.com

  1. On the top right of the browser click the Menu button then click the Options icon.
  2. Click Privacy & Security, scroll to Permissions, and leave the Block pop-up windows box checked, then click the Exceptions button to the right of that option.
  3. In the Address of web site field, type https://text.uwbookstore.com/

    Click Allow and the URL will appear in the list of allowed sites.
  4. Click Save changes

Enabling Pop-up Windows in Safari

Pop-ups must be allowed in your web browser and other installed pop-up blocking programs including third-party toolbars (e.g., Google, Yahoo!, AOL) for the University Book Store to display course content, important messages, and Help.

To allow pop-ups in Safari for text.uwbookstore.com

  1. In Safari, navigate to https://text.uwbookstore.com/, open the Safari menu and select Preferences.
  2. Click the Websites tab.
  3. Click on Pop-up Windows in the General menu.

    Then click on "Block and Notify" in the Currently Open Websites menu, and select "Allow"
  4. Close the Preferences window.

Enabling Pop-up Windows in Chrome

By default, Google Chrome blocks pop-ups from automatically showing up on your screen. When a pop-up is blocked, the address bar will be marked Pop-up blocked .

If you see the Pop-up blocked image in the address bar:
  1. Click on the icon to open up a dialog box.
  2. Click the radio button for "Always allow pop-ups and redirects from https://text.uwbookstore.com/
  3. Click Done

 

To allow pop-ups in Chrome for text.uwbookstore.com

  1. At the top right, click on the three dots and then Settings.
  2. Under "Privacy and security," click Site settings.
  3. Click Pop-ups and redirects.
  4. Click on the Add button next to Allow.
  5. Enter https://text.uwbookstore.com/ in the Add a site box, and then click Add

Enabling Pop-up Windows in Microsoft Edge

Pop-ups must be allowed in your web browser and other installed pop-up blocking programs including third-party toolbars (e.g., Google, Yahoo!, AOL) for the University Book Store to display course content, important messages, and Help.

  1. At the top right, click on the three dots and then Settings.
  2. Under Settings click Site permissions, then click Pop-ups and redirects.
  3. Click on the Add button next to Allow.
  4. Enter https://text.uwbookstore.com/ in the Add a site box, and then click Add.
