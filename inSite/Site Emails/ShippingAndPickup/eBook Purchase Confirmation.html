To access your eBook(s):

  1. Navigate to http://text.uwbookstore.com/login
  2. Log in with your email address and password.
  3. Click on Activate and Access eBooks.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to activate and access the eBooks you have purchased*.
 

To access your CEI/Campus eBookstore Code(s):

  1. Navigate to https://campusebookstore.com/redeem/
  2. Follow the prompts to redeem your code(s)
 

RedShelf books are automatically activated when purchased online. Simply navigate to http://text.uwbookstore.com/login, login with your email address and password, and click on Activate and Access eBooks. Your book will be displayed under the heading, "Other Digital Titles".

 

*You will need to disable your browser's popup blocker to access your eBooks. View Instructions Here

No returns once activated.
