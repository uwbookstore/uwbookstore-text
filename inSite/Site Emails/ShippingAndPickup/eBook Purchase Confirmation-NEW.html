Accessing your eBook(s):

Please note the ebook Vendor listed above.
The instructions for accessing ebooks for the different vendors are listed below.

IF your eBook(s) is from CEI/Campus eBookstore

  1. Navigate to https://campusebookstore.com/redeem/
  2. Follow the prompts to redeem your alpha-numeric code(s)

There may be a short delay to gain access to your material. You will receive two emails-the 2nd email may be delayed if the provider's system is too busy. Your item will be processed when it's your turn. Once it's completed - you will receive another email with all your details on how to access your item.

If you have not received the 2nd email, we suggest you check your junk or spam folders.

IF your eBook(s) is from RedShelf *:

RedShelf books are automatically activated when purchased online.

  1. Navigate to http://text.uwbookstore.com/login
  2. Login with your email address and password.
  3. Click on Activate and Access eBooks.
  4. Your book will be displayed under the heading, "Other Digital Titles".
 

IF Your eBook(s) is from VitalSource *:

  1. Navigate to http://text.uwbookstore.com/login
  2. Log in with your email address and password.
  3. Click on Activate and Access eBooks.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to activate and access the eBooks you have purchased*.
 
 

*You will need to enable pop-ups for text.uwbookstore.com in your browser.
View detailed instructions for enabling pop-ups here.
